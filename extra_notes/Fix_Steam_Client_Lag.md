# Fix Steam Client Lag in Window Managers such as Sway and/or i3

### **Step #1:**
Navigate to **"Settings"**

### **Step #2:**
Click **"Interface"**

### **Step #3:**
**Uncheck option**
- Enable GPU accelerated rendering in web views (requires restart)
